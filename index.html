Top Ten Signs You Have Been Spending Too Much Time Web Surfing

  1. You've become very territorial about your computer
  2. It's been months since you've had a conversation with a real person (face-to-face)
  3. You get nervous if you're going somewhere without Wi-Fi
  4. You feel an overwhelming sense of relief when you leave a Wi-Fi dead zone
  5. The first thing you do when you're planning a trip is research the Wi-Fi situation
  6. The only thing you clean is your browser history
  7. You've said things online that you would never, ever say in person
  8. Your worst fear is a black screen and a lost charger
  9. You've started referencing memes as if they're universal common knowledge
  10. You've lost days staring at your laptop
